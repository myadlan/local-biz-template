How to Pin on Waze

Save our location to your Waze so you can navigate here anytime — even offline.

1
Tap "Open in Waze" below
This opens Waze and drops a pin at our exact location.
2
Tap the pin on the map
A popup will appear at the bottom of your screen with the location name.
3
Tap "Save" or "Add to favourites"
The pin is now saved. Find it anytime under My Waze → Saved Places.
4
Navigate anytime
Tap the saved place and hit "Go" — Waze will guide you with live traffic.
